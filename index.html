About L&T Crestoria Estate

L&T Crestoria Estate is an upcoming gated community in Panvel, offering 2, 3 & 4 BHK homes with scenic forest and hill views. The residences feature spacious layouts with grand decks for ample light and ventilation, along with premium finishes and fittings.

Located in a prime area, the project is close to key landmarks like Amity University, Dmart, and major hospitals. It offers excellent connectivity via Mumbai Pune Expressway, with the Navi Mumbai International Airport just 15 minutes away.
